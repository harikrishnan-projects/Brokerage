Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about pricing, charges and equity delivery.

Do you have hidden charges?

No. We follow a transparent pricing model. All fees such as brokerage, exchange charges, taxes and any statutory levies are shown at the point of order and on trade confirmations. Any promotional offers will be clearly communicated.

How is brokerage calculated?

Brokerage depends on the plan you choose. We offer flat fees per executed order and special rates for Pro and Elite plans. Brokerage for each trade is displayed in the order confirmation and on your billing statements.

What taxes will be applied?

Standard taxes and statutory charges may apply, such as GST (where applicable), exchange fees, SEBI charges and stamp duty per your jurisdiction. These are shown separately on the trade invoice.

Is equity delivery free?

Equity delivery is free for most account types as described in our pricing overview. Some special or promotional accounts may vary — always check the plan details.
