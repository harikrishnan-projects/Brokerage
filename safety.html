Data Security & Encryption

We protect your personal and trading data with industry-standard encryption, secure access controls and continuous monitoring — so you can trade with confidence.

256-bit SSL Encryption

All data in transit is protected using strong 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption to prevent eavesdropping.

Secure Login & MFA

Multi-factor authentication (OTP / authenticator apps) and strong password policies protect account access.

Biometric Login (optional)

Fingerprint and face recognition support for supported devices — fast and secure optional sign-in.

Encrypted Data Storage

Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 and access is limited by role-based controls.

Session Timeout & Fraud Detection

Automatic session expiry, device recognition and real-time monitoring to detect suspicious activity promptly.

Why it matters

Security measures protect your funds and personal information, reduce fraud risk, and build trust with users.

Funds Protection & Safety of Assets

We keep your money and securities safe with clear segregation, banking standards, and transparent policies.

Segregated Client Accounts

Client funds are held separately from company operating accounts to prevent commingling.

Bank-grade Fund Storage

Funds stored with regulated banking partners using industry-standard custody arrangements.

No Misuse of Client Securities

Client securities are never rehypothecated without explicit consent and are accounted for daily.

Transparent Margin Policies

Clear margin requirements and calls published in advance — no hidden leverage or surprise liquidations.

Insurance Coverage

Where available, insurance policies provide additional protection for client assets against operational risks.

Why it matters

These safeguards prevent the loss or misuse of investor funds, increase transparency and strengthen confidence in our services.
